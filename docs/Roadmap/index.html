v3.0 — Next Up

Every club, its own command

Second and third clubs onboard independently. Riders carry memberships across clubs. Each club keeps its own data, identity, and branding.

v2.0 — Tactical Android

The live ride goes mobile

A live tactical map showing every rider's position in real time. The Support Beacon puts help one tap away. Captain controls from the road.

v4.0 — Ride Depth

Complexity without chaos

Sub-group Captains for large pelotons. Multiple simultaneous rides. Mid-ride SAG reassignment. Advanced scheduling for every recurrence pattern.

v5.0 — Club Growth

The club comes alive

Observer role, member-uploaded routes, ride history on every profile, and Voice of Customer built into the product.

v6.0 — Intelligence

Data that works for your club

Strava sync, club analytics, advanced scheduling, and Velo Mode.
