# VEcheLOn Copy Spec: Situational Value Grid (Updated)

## 01. PLANNING
- **VISUAL:** GPX path icon with Start/End waypoints.
- **The Problem:** Route Fragmentation. Inconsistent sharing and manual setup of ride markers.
- **The Value:** Pinned GPX. Admins pin a route; the system auto-parses Start, End, and Coffee stops.
- **VEcheLOn Outcome:** Zero-Admin Setup. Riders get the Garmin file; the map gets the tactical data.

## 02. ROLLOUT
- **VISUAL:** Group "Radar" lock-on icon.
- **The Problem:** The Vanishing Group. Once a ride starts, fragmentation happens instantly. In Gran Fondos, club members are swallowed by the crowd.
- **The Value:** Passive Participation. Single-tap "Start" for members; instant QR scan for guests.
- **VEcheLOn Outcome:** Seamless Entry. A real-time roster reconciles "Expected" vs "Active" in seconds.

## 03. THE RIDE
- **VISUAL:** "Sweeping" radar icon with rider detection.
- **The Problem:** Loss of Contact. High-speed pace changes or third-party event traffic make it impossible to gauge the group’s relative position.
- **The Value:** Tactical Presence. Real-time state mapping: Moving, Stopped (5-min Halo), or Inactive (15-min Fade).
- **VEcheLOn Outcome:** Relative Positioning. Maintain a distinct club unit and visual awareness even in a crowd of thousands.

## 04. SUPPORT
- **VISUAL:** Tactical "Targeting" crosshair with aid indicator.
- **The Problem:** Blind Sweeping. The Support Van cannot efficiently locate or contact riders in distress.
- **The Value:** Intercept Logic. Direct navigation to Halo icons with One-Click Calling.
- **VEcheLOn Outcome:** Direct Intercept. Rapid, informed aid via one-tap phone links to the rider.

## 05. AFTERMATH
- **VISUAL:** "Digital Shredder" scanline icon.
- **The Problem:** Data Toxicity. Privacy risks from long-term storage of sensitive GPS breadcrumbs.
- **The Value:** Selective Hard Purge. Automated scrub of tactical data exactly 4 hours post-ride.
- **VEcheLOn Outcome:** Zero-Footprint Safety. Club history is preserved; sensitive tracks are erased forever.
