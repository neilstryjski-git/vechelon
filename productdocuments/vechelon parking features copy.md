# VEcheLOn Copy Spec: Situational Value Grid (Updated)

## 01. PLANNING
- **VISUAL:** GPX path icon with Start/End waypoints.
- **The Problem:** Ad-hoc Routes. Newer riders are often unfamiliar with the course, leading to fragmentation and uncertainty during the ride.
- **The Value:** Official Route Locking. Admins pin a GPX route and communicate clear start/stop points to build group confidence.
- **VEcheLOn Outcome:** Consistent Group Rides. A pre-defined tactical map ensures every rider knows the plan, regardless of experience level.

## 02. ROLLOUT
- **VISUAL:** Group "Radar" lock-on icon.
- **The Problem:** Natural Fragmentation. This happens instantly in mixed-ability groups, where pace differences can swallow riders and make it hard to maintain the club unit.
- **The Value:** Group Persistence. A single-tap "Start" keeps every member on the tactical roster, no matter their speed.
- **VEcheLOn Outcome:** Unified Group Presence. Real-time reconciliation of the active roster ensures no rider is left behind or lost in the crowd.

## 03. THE RIDE
- **VISUAL:** "Sweeping" radar icon with rider detection.
- **The Problem:** Loss of Contact. High-speed pace changes and third-party event traffic make it impossible to gauge the group’s relative position—especially in Gran Fondos where thousands of riders can swallow the club unit.
- **The Value:** Tactical Presence. Real-time state mapping: Moving, Stopped (5-min Halo), or Inactive (15-min Fade).
- **VEcheLOn Outcome:** Relative Positioning. Maintain a distinct club identity and visual awareness even in a crowd of thousands.

## 04. SUPPORT
- **VISUAL:** Tactical "Targeting" crosshair with aid indicator.
- **The Problem:** Off-the-Pack Fragmentation. Support vans cannot efficiently locate or contact riders who have dropped off or had an incident away from the main group.
- **The Value:** Direct Communication. Real-time tactical icons for isolated riders include one-click calling and pre-populated contact info for the support team.
- **VEcheLOn Outcome:** Informed Intercept. Rapid, targeted assistance via one-tap phone links and precise situational mapping.

## 05. AFTERMATH
- **VISUAL:** "Digital Shredder" scanline icon.
- **The Problem:** Responsible Data. Privacy risks from long-term storage of sensitive GPS breadcrumbs.
- **The Value:** Responsible Data Hygiene. Automated scrub of tactical data exactly 4 hours post-ride.
- **VEcheLOn Outcome:** Zero-Footprint Safety. Club history is preserved; sensitive tracks are erased forever.
